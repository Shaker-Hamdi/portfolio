Renault

Renault is a mobile app I did while I was working as Art Director and front-end developer in my previous company "Adsia". It was supposed to be the official app for Renault Egypt at the time, but unfortunately due to some major problems the company had to shut down. I really hope some day the company will resume its business and this project would see the light.

In this project I did a set of custom icons to suite the company's unique look and feel of the app.
